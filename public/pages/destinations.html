Our Destinations

From the snow-capped summit of Kilimanjaro to the turquoise shores of Zanzibar — Tanzania holds wonders for every traveller.

Trekking

Mount Kilimanjaro

⏱ 7 days 📍 Kilimanjaro Region

Africa's highest peak at 5,895m. Ascend through five climate zones — rainforest, heathland, moorland, alpine desert, and arctic summit — on a guided climb with our expert team.

Serengeti National Park

⏱ 4–6 days 📍 Mara Region

Home to the world-famous Great Migration — over 1.5 million wildebeest crossing the plains. Spot lions, elephants, and cheetahs in one of Africa's greatest wildlife spectacles.

Beach & Culture

Zanzibar Island

⏱ 5–7 days 📍 Zanzibar Archipelago

The Spice Island — ancient Stone Town's UNESCO-listed alleys, white powder beaches at Nungwi, snorkelling with sea turtles, and fresh seafood grilled on the beach at sunset.

Wildlife

Ngorongoro Crater

⏱ 2–3 days 📍 Arusha Region

The world's largest intact volcanic caldera, sheltering over 25,000 large animals. See all of the Big Five — lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino — in one breathtaking bowl.

Culture

Dar es Salaam

⏱ 2–3 days 📍 Coast Region

Tanzania's vibrant commercial capital on the Indian Ocean coast. Explore the National Museum, Kariakoo Market, Kivukoni Fish Market, and the warm Swahili hospitality of the city streets.

Wildlife

Selous Game Reserve

⏱ 3–4 days 📍 Morogoro Region

Africa's largest game reserve and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Boat safaris on the Rufiji River, walking safaris, and fly camping under the stars far from any crowds or tourist circuits.

Culture

Maasai Mara Villages

⏱ 1–2 days 📍 Arusha Region

Visit a genuine Maasai boma (homestead) near Arusha. Learn about warrior traditions, ochre body decoration, beadwork, and cattle-herding culture that has endured for over 400 years.

Mafia Island

⏱ 4–5 days 📍 Pwani Region

Tanzania's hidden gem — a tiny island with some of the world's best whale shark diving, pristine coral gardens, deserted beaches, and an atmosphere untouched by mass tourism.

The smallest national park in Tanzania and home to Jane Goodall's famous chimpanzee research. Trek through dense forest to observe chimps in their natural habitat on the shores of Lake Tanganyika.
